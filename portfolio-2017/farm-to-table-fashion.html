An Industry Awakening

For a class at the New School, named Collaborative Journalism, a group of students and I worked on creating an article for the university newspaper, The New School Free Press. I was the lead designer on this project which included working heavily within Wordpress and using the CMS to our advantage. The piece, has a heavy focus on material, so the writers and I worked together on collaborating to create a piece that was representative of the story that they were trying to tell. I also helped on reporting for this piece.
